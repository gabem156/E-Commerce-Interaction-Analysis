## Tableau Dashboard

An interactive Tableau dashboard was created to visualize customer engagement behavior across an e-commerce platform.

### Dashboard Features

* KPI cards displaying:

  * Total Interactions
  * Total Likes
  * Total Views
  * Total Purchases

* Monthly Interaction Trend

  * Tracks engagement activity over time
  * Highlights seasonal spikes and engagement patterns

* Interactions by Type

  * Compares likes, views, and purchases
  * Identifies the most common user actions

* Monthly Engagement Distribution

  * Displays the composition of interactions by month
  * Shows how engagement types vary throughout the year

### Interactive Functionality

* Month filter allows users to dynamically explore engagement activity across different time periods.
* All visualizations update automatically based on filter selections.

### Key Insights

* Likes represented the largest share of user interactions.
* User engagement increased significantly during the final quarter of the year.
* Views and purchases followed similar growth patterns, suggesting stronger customer intent during peak periods.
* Engagement activity was highest in November and December.
